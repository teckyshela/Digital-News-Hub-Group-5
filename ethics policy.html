ETHICS POLICY

Preamble

Digital News Hub entails a moral code that contains guidelines for expectations, professional conduct and integrity which is aimed at; upholding established standards within performance grounds, guiding decision-making, promoting accountability and responsibility, fostering a positive and comprehensive platform, enhancing and maintaining reputation without breach or bias, providing a basis for effective enforcement and promoting continuous and consistent improvement in ethical behavior.

Our platform incorporates but is not limited to policies comprising fact checking, misinformation, privacy and copyright.

1.FACT CHECKING

Every written material must be based on grounds relating to seeking truth and reporting it.This entails checking and proving facts, choosing to be accurate as opposed to operating with speed, providing context for phenomena as well as avoiding stereotypes thereby providing a basis for a good flow within the platform.

Professionalism is highly regarded in the platform where conducting media coverage and activities is accorded with high standards of ethics, accountability in all undertakings, legality of material and ways of acquisition and credibility in terms of sources, while exercising rights such as freedom of expression and information is also considered.

Responsibility.A reporter under Digital News Hub is accountable to words said and actions taken and is thereby considered knowledgeable of the consequences that may arise from harmful practices such as defamation or any other unlawful acts under media code of conduct and laws of the country.

In our content creation,we ensured that the figures, examples and cases used are factual.In addressing AI trends for example,the references in numbers in usage and consumption of AI had more than one source attributing to closely the same figures.

2.MISINFORMATION

While taking into account that misinformation can occur without a deliberate intent to mislead, Digital News Hub is aware and cautious of the misinformation effect and the damages that may come along with it.

A reporter must therefore be conversant with beneficence-acts of kindness and nonmaleficience-avoiding harm and how to take approach.Neither should be used when writing stories as it may come with bias and trigger the misinformation effect.It is therefore an obligation to write or cover an accurate information on a person or a topic, backed by facts.

In our content creation,we referred to multiple sources across the internet and did not merely rely on what we have heard or have a little knowledge about.No section of the content in our platform is intended to mislead under any circumstances or context, but to inform as would any news outlet in whatever form.

3.PRIVACY

In compliance with the integrity and respect in collecting data, using the data and managing the user's personal data, Digital News Hub stipulates what data is collected and the reason for it,how the data is used,how it is accessed and who has access to it, how the data is protected and how the user can review and update this data.

Data in our platform is as confidential to us as it is to our users.Website data is available to users when visiting Digital News Hub, navigating to various locations by tapping the various tabs on our homepage.Personal data is accessible to third parties in cases of legal requirement and government processes.

4.COPYRIGHT

In line with the law of Copyright Act of 2001, which covers Protection of copyright and related rights, infringement and enforcement, exceptions and works in the public domain, collective management of copyright, and administrative issues, Digital News Hub has taken into account in the project issues such as:

Fair Use;for purposes such as criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, or research, is not an infringement of copyright.

Access to information;In Kenya, the Access to Information Act 2016 gives citizens the right to access information held by the state or other people. The law also requires the government to publish important information in the public interest.

Copyright protection;legal system protects the rights of creators over their original works. It prevents others from:

copying the work,distributing copies of the work, renting or lending copies of the work,performing, showing, or playing the work in public,making an adaptation of the work or putting the work on the internet.

Protection period for the life of the author plus 50 years and the period of 50 years from the date of publication if author is unknown,as well as the joint authorship is also cknowledged by Digital News Hub.

In our content creation, caution has been instrumental in matters copyright, such that there is no copying the work,distributing copies of the work, renting or lending copies of the work,performing, showing, or playing the work in public,making an adaptation of the work or putting the work on the internet. However,we have put to great use,the law of fair use in other people's content for purposes of criticism, commenting, writing our articles and in research, including photos and videos used which is not infringement of copyright.
